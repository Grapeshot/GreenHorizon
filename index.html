What Can You Do?

As energy demand continues to rise - driven by AI, cloud computing, and digital infrastructure - the choices we make about power sources matter more than ever.

  • Stay informed: Track your local energy mix and advocate for renewable investments
  • Support green data centers: Choose cloud providers committed to 100% renewable energy
  • Push for policy change: Contact representatives to support clean energy incentives
  • Reduce consumption: Small efficiency gains multiplied across the population can make a huge difference.
